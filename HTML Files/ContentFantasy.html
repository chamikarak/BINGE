    • THE WITCHER

    "Evil is evil...lesser, greater, milling. It's all the same."  ~Geralt of Rivia

    Genre: Fantasy, Serial drama, Adventure, Action
    Starring: Henry Cavill, Freya Allan, Eamon Farren, Anya Chalotra, Joey Batey, MyAnna Buring, Royce Pierreson, Mimi Ndiweni, Wilson Radjou-Pujalte, Anna Shaffer and Mahesh Jadu
    No. of seasons: 1
    Running-time: 47-67 minutes
    Premire Date: December 20th 2019
    Air-time: 2019-present
    Original network: Netflix

    The Witcher is a drama series based on the book series of the same name by Andrzei Sapkowski. The story is set in a fictional land known as 'The Continent' and explores the legend of Geralt of Rivia and princess Ciri. The main three protagonists initially are shown in different timelines, each facing their own challenges, before eventually coming together

    The next season of The Witcher is scheduled to be realeased in 2021, so that gives you plently of time to catch up!

    GAME OF THRONES

    "When you play the game of thrones, you win or you die."  ~Cersie Lannister

    Genre: Fantasy
    Starring: Nikolaj Coster-Waldau, Michelle Fairley, Lena Headey, Emillia Clarke, Iain Glen, Kit Harington, Sophie Turner, Maisie Williams, Richard Madden, Isaac Hempstead, Peter Dinklage, Aiden Gillen, John Bradley, Stephen Dillane, Carice van Houten, Jerome Flynn, Natalie Dormer and Charles Dance
    No. of seasons: 8
    Running-time: 50-82 minutes
    Premire Date: Aril 17th 2011
    Air-time: 2011-2019
    Original network: HBO

    Game of Thrones is a fantasy drama series that is a modification of A song of Fire and Ice, by George R. Martin' series of fantasy novels. The series follow a medivial world of the nobel houses of seven kingdoms at war for the Iron Throne, the highest position in the land.

    The series was filmed and produced in Belfast and other areas in the United Kingdom, as well as Canada, Croatia, Iceland, Morroco, Malta and Span.
