    • PRETTY LITTLE LIARS

    "Never trust a pretty girl with an ugly secret."  ~A

    Genre: Crime thriller, Mystery, Teen drama
    Starring: Troian Bellisario, Ashley Benson, Holly Marie Combs, Lucy Hale Ian Harding, Bianca Lawson, Laura Leighton, Chad Lowe, Shay Mitchell, Nia Peeples, Sasha Pieterse, Tyler Blackburn, Janel Parrish and Andrea Parker
    No. of seasons: 7
    Running-time: 41-45 minutes
    Premire Date: June 8th 2010
    Air-time: 2010-2017
    Original network: ABC Family/Freeform

    Pretty Liitle Liars is loosely based off of a series of books of the same name written by Sara Shepard. The story follows the lives four high-school girls (Aria, Hanna, Spenser and Emily) in the town Rosewood who's friendship falls apart when the leader of their clique, Allison, mysteriously disappears. One year later, the firends are forced to reunite when they begin recieving messages from an unknown figure who calls themselves "A". "A" threatens to expose their deepest darkest secrets if they refuse to do thier bidding; secrets about them that only the still missing Allison was supposed to know!

    If you like a good mystery, romance, suspense and epic plottwists, this is the TV series for you!

    Riverdale is a teen drama series that puts a dark twist to the beloved comic series Archie Comics. While the main charachters of the story (such as Archie, Veronica, Jughead and Betty) remain the same, the series follow the events that occured in the aftermath of the disappearance of high-school student Jason Blossom, while also protraying the inner struggle of the rest of the charachters as they go on with thier lives. Riverdale seems like a picture-perfect small town, but it has hidden darkness lingering within it, especially in the hearts of its people.

    Riverdale is far from the bright and funny world of Archie Comics, but if you enjoy dark secrets, numerous conflicts and a LOT of drama, why not give it a try?
